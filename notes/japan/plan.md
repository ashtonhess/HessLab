# North Fuji / Deep Lakes Driving Plan

Use this as the day-of plan for the **North Fuji / Deep Lakes Loop**.

- **Car pickup:** `10:00 AM`
- **Pickup location:** [OnlyJDM Haneda Main Store](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=OnlyJDM+Haneda+Main+Store%2C+2+Chome-20-14+Omoriminami%2C+Ota+City%2C+Tokyo+143-0013)

## Before Pickup

- Bring **passport**, **valid International Driving Permit**, home driver's license, credit card, and enough cash for small rural stops.
- Confirm with `OnlyJDM` that overnight parking in `Shinjuku` and next-morning return is acceptable.
- Check `Chuo Expressway` traffic before committing west.
- Check Fuji visibility before leaving Tokyo. If Fuji is fully hidden all day, this is still usable as a village/cave/lake drive, but the viewpoint stops should stay short.
- Set the full route in Google Maps before leaving the shop.
- Keep phone charging active all day.

## Hard Timing Rules

- **Leave Oishi Park by:** `2:25 PM`
- **Leave Nenba by:** `4:00 PM`
- **Leave Narusawa Ice Cave by:** `4:50 PM`
- **Leave Lake Shoji by:** `6:10 PM`
- **If you are 45+ minutes late after Oishi Park:** keep Nenba and Ice Cave, but make Lake Motosu and Lake Shoji quick photo stops.
- **If you are 60+ minutes late after Narusawa Ice Cave:** skip Lake Shoji and go directly toward `Shinjuku` after Lake Motosu.
- **Do not add stops after Lake Shoji.**

## Day Plan

### Leg 1 — Pickup To Tenka Chaya

- **Time:** `10:00 AM-1:00 PM`
- **Drive:** about `3h`
- **Map:** [OnlyJDM Haneda -> Tenka Chaya](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=OnlyJDM+Haneda+Main+Store%2C+2+Chome-20-14+Omoriminami%2C+Ota+City%2C+Tokyo+143-0013&destination=Tenka+Chaya%2C+2739+Kawaguchi%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0304&travelmode=driving)
- **Destination:** [Tenka Chaya / Misaka Pass](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Tenka+Chaya%2C+2739+Kawaguchi%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0304)

What to do:

- Pick up the car at `OnlyJDM Haneda`.
- Leave Tokyo and drive toward the Fuji Five Lakes area.
- Use this as the main outbound push. Do not add a random stop unless you need fuel, restroom, or food.
- On the final approach, drive calmly. The pass road is scenic and winding.

At Tenka Chaya:

- **Planned stop:** `1:00-1:35 PM`
- **What we are doing:** Take in the elevated `Mt. Fuji + Lake Kawaguchi` view, stretch, use bathrooms if available, and get tea/snack or a quick light meal if timing works.
- **Hours:** commonly `10:00 AM-5:00 PM`; winter/weather closures can happen.
- **Parking:** small lot, about `10 passenger cars` at the main teahouse.
- **Cost:** viewpoint is free; food/drinks optional.
- **Do not linger past:** `1:35 PM`

### Leg 2 — Tenka Chaya To Oishi Park

- **Time:** `1:35-2:00 PM`
- **Drive:** about `25m`
- **Map:** [Tenka Chaya -> Oishi Park](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Tenka+Chaya%2C+2739+Kawaguchi%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0304&destination=Oishi+Park%2C+2585+Oishi%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0305&travelmode=driving)
- **Destination:** [Oishi Park](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Oishi+Park%2C+2585+Oishi%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0305)

What to do:

- Drop down from the pass toward Lake Kawaguchi.
- Park at Oishi Park / Kawaguchiko Natural Living Center.

At Oishi Park:

- **Planned stop:** `2:00-2:25 PM`
- **What we are doing:** Walk to the lakefront view, take the clean postcard photos of `Mt. Fuji + Lake Kawaguchi`, then leave.
- **Parking:** free parking; Oishi Park has a large lot.
- **Cost:** free.
- **Bathrooms / cafe:** available around the Natural Living Center area.
- **Do not let this become a long stop.**
- **Leave by:** `2:25 PM`

### Leg 3 — Oishi Park To Saiko Iyashi-no-Sato Nenba

- **Time:** `2:25-3:10 PM`
- **Drive:** about `45m`
- **Map:** [Oishi Park -> Saiko Iyashi-no-Sato Nenba](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Oishi+Park%2C+2585+Oishi%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0305&destination=Saiko+Iyashi-no-Sato+Nenba%2C+2710+Saiko+Nenba%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0332&travelmode=driving)
- **Destination:** [Saiko Iyashi-no-Sato Nenba](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Saiko+Iyashi-no-Sato+Nenba%2C+2710+Saiko+Nenba%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0332)

What to do:

- Drive west from Kawaguchiko into the quieter Lake Saiko area.
- This is where the route changes from standard Fuji viewpoints to a quieter cultural stop.

At Nenba:

- **Planned stop:** `3:10-4:00 PM`
- **What we are doing:** Enter the thatched-roof village, walk the main area, take photos, and use this as the main food/rest stop if food is available.
- **Hours:** March-November `9:00 AM-5:00 PM`; last entry `4:30 PM`.
- **Admission:** adults `JPY 500`; elementary/junior high children `JPY 250`.
- **Parking:** free.
- **Important:** some shops may close independently even when the village is open.
- **Leave by:** `4:00 PM`

### Leg 4 — Nenba To Narusawa Ice Cave

- **Time:** `4:00-4:25 PM`
- **Drive:** about `25m`
- **Map:** [Saiko Iyashi-no-Sato Nenba -> Narusawa Ice Cave](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Saiko+Iyashi-no-Sato+Nenba%2C+2710+Saiko+Nenba%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0332&destination=Narusawa+Ice+Cave%2C+8533+Narusawa%2C+Yamanashi+401-0320&travelmode=driving)
- **Destination:** [Narusawa Ice Cave](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Narusawa+Ice+Cave%2C+8533+Narusawa%2C+Yamanashi+401-0320)

What to do:

- Drive from Lake Saiko toward the Aokigahara / Narusawa cave area.
- Keep this leg direct. Do not add the Wind Cave unless you intentionally give up later lake time.

At Narusawa Ice Cave:

- **Planned stop:** `4:25-4:50 PM`
- **What we are doing:** Buy tickets, do the short cave loop, then get back to the car.
- **Admission:** adults `JPY 350`; children `JPY 200`.
- **Hours:** vary by season; April is commonly listed around `9:00 AM-5:00 PM`, with some sources showing seasonal variation. Confirm same-day closing time before entering Nenba.
- **Important cave conditions:** cold, wet, slippery, low ceilings, narrow sections.
- **Entry limits:** children under `100 cm` may not be allowed inside; pets are not allowed inside.
- **Do not enter** if anyone is uncomfortable with tight cave spaces.
- **Leave by:** `4:50 PM`

### Leg 5 — Narusawa Ice Cave To Lake Motosuko Observation Park

- **Time:** `4:50-5:20 PM`
- **Drive:** about `30m`
- **Map:** [Narusawa Ice Cave -> Lake Motosuko Observation Park](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Narusawa+Ice+Cave%2C+8533+Narusawa%2C+Yamanashi+401-0320&destination=Lake+Motosuko+Observation+Park%2C+Nakanokura%2C+Minobu%2C+Yamanashi+409-3104&travelmode=driving)
- **Destination:** [Lake Motosuko Observation Park](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Lake+Motosuko+Observation+Park%2C+Nakanokura%2C+Minobu%2C+Yamanashi+409-3104)

What to do:

- Drive west to Lake Motosu.
- This is the deepest and most remote-feeling lake stop.
- Use the north-shore observation / parking area for the quick view.

At Lake Motosu:

- **Planned stop:** `5:20-5:40 PM`
- **What we are doing:** Quick lakefront view/photo stop. This is the `1,000-yen bill` Fuji-lake area.
- **Cost:** free.
- **Important:** do **not** drive onto rough lakeside ground or improvised shoulders. Use paved or clearly designated parking only.
- **Do not hike to Nakanokura Pass.** This plan only has time for the lakefront/parking-area view.
- **Leave by:** `5:40 PM`

### Leg 6 — Lake Motosu To Lake Shoji

- **Time:** `5:40-5:55 PM`
- **Drive:** about `15m`
- **Map:** [Lake Motosuko Observation Park -> Lake Shoji](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Lake+Motosuko+Observation+Park%2C+Nakanokura%2C+Minobu%2C+Yamanashi+409-3104&destination=Lake+Shoji%2C+Shoji%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0336&travelmode=driving)
- **Destination:** [Lake Shoji](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Lake+Shoji%2C+Shoji%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0336)

What to do:

- Make the short move from Lake Motosu to Lake Shoji.
- This is the final scenic stop before the return drive.

At Lake Shoji:

- **Planned stop:** `5:55-6:10 PM`
- **What we are doing:** Quick final lake view, photo, and route check before committing to the Tokyo return.
- **Cost:** free.
- **Important:** keep this stop short. This is a closing note, not a long activity stop.
- **Leave by:** `6:10 PM`

### Leg 7 — Lake Shoji To Shinjuku Overnight Parking

- **Time:** `6:10-8:00 PM+`
- **Drive:** about `1h 50m+`, depending on Chuo return traffic
- **Primary map:** [Lake Shoji -> Public Shinjuku Parking](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Lake+Shoji%2C+Shoji%2C+Fujikawaguchiko%2C+Yamanashi+401-0336&destination=Public+Shinjuku+Parking%2C+2-10-1+Kabukicho%2C+Shinjuku-ku%2C+Tokyo+160-0021&travelmode=driving)
- **Primary parking destination:** [Public Shinjuku Parking](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Public+Shinjuku+Parking%2C+2-10-1+Kabukicho%2C+Shinjuku-ku%2C+Tokyo+160-0021)
- **West-side backup parking:** [Times Nishi-Shinjuku 4-chome No. 4](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Times+Nishi-Shinjuku+4-chome+No.4%2C+4-21+Nishi-Shinjuku%2C+Shinjuku-ku%2C+Tokyo)

What to do:

- Leave the Fuji area and return toward Tokyo.
- Do not add dinner or sightseeing before parking the car.
- If traffic is bad, keep driving and eat after parking in `Shinjuku`.

At Shinjuku:

- Park first.
- Remove valuables from the car.
- Take photos of the car condition and parking location.
- Keep the parking ticket/receipt safe.
- Confirm next-morning route and timing back to `OnlyJDM Haneda`.

## If The Day Starts Going Wrong

### If Chuo Expressway is already bad leaving Tokyo

- Keep the route only if Google Maps still shows arrival at Tenka Chaya close to `1:00 PM`.
- If arrival is much later than `1:30 PM`, decide immediately whether the Fuji route is still worth it.

### If Fuji is not visible

- Keep `Nenba` and `Narusawa Ice Cave`.
- Make `Tenka Chaya`, `Oishi Park`, `Lake Motosu`, and `Lake Shoji` shorter.

### If Nenba is missed or closed

- Go directly to `Narusawa Ice Cave`.
- Use the saved time for a calmer Lake Motosu / Lake Shoji finish.

### If Narusawa Ice Cave is closed

- Skip it.
- Continue directly to `Lake Motosu`.
- Do not add another cave or attraction unless it is clearly open and does not delay the `6:10 PM` return target.

### If return traffic is bad

- Do not stop for dinner before Shinjuku.
- Park first, then eat.
- Keep enough energy for careful city driving at the end of the day.

## End State

- Car is parked overnight in `Shinjuku`.
- Valuables are removed.
- Parking ticket is saved.
- Next-morning return to `OnlyJDM Haneda` is planned.
- Do not treat the car return as solved until `OnlyJDM` has confirmed next-morning return is acceptable.
